    Button wires
    Type BCM GPIO numbers here, not the physical pin number on the 40-pin header. Wire changes need a restart.
    Your EC11 wiring: A on physical pin 29 = enter GPIO5. B on physical pin 31 = enter GPIO6. C goes to physical pin 30 GND. If the knob push is on physical pin 33, enter GPIO13.
